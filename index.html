About ICECE

ICECE is an international conference hosted at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), bringing together cutting-edge research across electrical, electronic, and computer engineering. The conference features keynote talks, peer-reviewed technical sessions, poster presentations, and industry engagement.

Scope

Covers theory, design, implementation, and real-world deployment across major ECE domains, with a focus on research impact and practical relevance.

Research Community

A platform for postgraduate students, early-career researchers, and senior experts to connect through sessions, Q&A, and networking opportunities.

Industry & Innovation

Bridges academia and industry through invited talks, panels, and demonstrations, supporting technology transfer and collaboration.

Call for Papers

ICECE 2026 invites original, unpublished research contributions in Electrical and Computer Engineering. Submissions will undergo peer review and accepted papers will be scheduled for presentation during the conference.

Conference Tracks

ICECE 2026 invites submissions across a broad range of Electrical and Computer Engineering research areas. Accepted papers will be organized into the following thematic tracks.

Track 1: Algorithms, Artificial Intelligence and Data Science

  • Algorithms and Computation
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Cloud Computing & Big Data
  • Biometrics
  • Software & Database Systems
  • Data Mining and Data Analytics
  • Deep Learning and Reinforcement Learning
  • Explainable AI

Track 2: Computer Systems, Networks and Security

  • Computer Architecture & Systems
  • Computer Networks and Security
  • Embedded and Intelligent Systems & IoT
  • Cyber-Physical Systems
  • Edge and Fog Computing
  • Distributed Systems

Track 3: Signal Processing, Computer Vision and Human–Computer Interaction

  • Signal, Image & Video Processing
  • Computer Vision, Graphics & HCI
  • Multimedia Processing
  • Speech and Natural Language Processing
  • Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR)

Track 4: Communication Systems and Networks

  • Telecommunication Theory and Systems
  • Wireless Communication and Networks
  • Optical Communication and Networks
  • 5G/6G Communication Systems
  • Satellite and Space Communications
  • Internet of Everything

Track 5: Electronic Devices, Materials and Photonics

  • Electronic and Photonic Materials
  • Electronic Devices
  • Optoelectronic Devices
  • Nanophotonics and Plasmonics
  • Photonic Technologies & Applications
  • Quantum Devices

Track 6: VLSI, Nanotechnology and Microsystems

  • VLSI Circuits & Systems
  • Nanotechnology and MEMS
  • Sensor Technologies and Applications
  • Analog and Mixed Signal Circuits
  • Integrated Microsystems

Track 7: Power, Energy and Smart Grid Technologies

  • Electrical Machines & Drives
  • Power Electronics
  • Power Systems
  • Smart Grids
  • Renewable Energy
  • Photovoltaic Systems
  • Energy Storage Systems
  • Electric Vehicles and Charging Infrastructure

Track 8: Robotics, Automation, Biomedical and Emerging Technologies

  • Robotics, Control & Automation
  • Biomedical Engineering & Bioinformatics
  • Emerging Technologies
  • Engineering Education Focusing on 4IR
  • Autonomous Systems
  • Intelligent Healthcare Systems

Venue & Travel

ICECE 2026 will be hosted at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), Dhaka. Dhaka is the capital of Bangladesh and a major hub for academia, industry, and innovation.

Venue

6th Floor, ECE Building
Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering
Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET)
Palashi, Dhaka-1000, Bangladesh

  • Main technical sessions will be held on the 6th floor of the ECE Building
  • Registration and help desk will be available near the conference entrance
  • Directional signage will be provided across the BUET campus

Accommodation

  • InterContinental Dhaka — Shahbag (≈1.5 km from BUET)
  • Pan Pacific Sonargaon — Karwan Bazar (≈3 km from BUET)
  • Hotel Sarina — Banani (≈7 km from BUET)
  • Amari Dhaka — Gulshan (≈8 km from BUET)
  • FARS Hotel & Resorts — Purana Paltan (≈3 km from BUET)

The most convenient way to travel to the conference venue is by ride-sharing services such as Uber or Pathao. Travel time from most recommended hotels ranges from 10–30 minutes depending on traffic conditions.
